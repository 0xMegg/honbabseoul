## Retrospective

**One-line summary**: Read the guide document -> initialize template -> apply in practice -> immediately incorporate improvements discovered along the way — ran this cycle 8 times in one night.

**What went well**:

- The 3-Role Workflow was the key. The structure where the Developer doesn't commit and the Reviewer commits only after final approval clearly works as a quality gate.
- Converting documents to English was a good decision. Korean interpretation costs accumulating across Claude sessions add up to significant waste.
- The carry-over field and decision-log are structural solutions to the "context loss" problem. They minimize information loss between sessions.
